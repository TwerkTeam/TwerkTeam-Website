Partner Program

by PixelCrashers on Feb 19th, 2018

TWERK TEAM!
Have you heard the rumblings? The whispers in the street? The faint sound of quivering anticipation on the air? Of course you have! And if not, where have you been!? Remember to check our Discord #announcements for all the exciting new things that are being rolled out this year as and when they occur! But in the meantime, let’s talk business. the Twerk Team Partner Program has been officially launched, and it’s amazing to see some of the Twerk Team already working towards, and even becoming a member of this Elite group of streamers.

For those of you that aren’t in the know on what the Partner Program means, and how you obtain this legendary symbol of awesomeness, don’t worry guys and gals, we’ve got you covered. Above all else, The Twerk Team is about us all making a difference to one another’s lives, and helping each other achieve goals. Through the Partner Program, you will both benefit others and in turn be benefited through mutual acts of community support which you’ll be able to demonstrate your completion of.

“So what do I have to do?” It’s simple – To become a Twerk Partner, you have to tick off and demonstrate via screenshots or otherwise, the following:

  • Add “TwerkTeam” to your communities on Twitch. [https://twitch.tv/communities/twerkteam]

  • Add a Twerk Team Banner to your Twitch Overlay or Panels (Found in #resources on Discord)

  • Tweet about us! Tag @TwerkTeamHQ and share what you enjoy about our Twerk Team and how can others join (provide link to http://twerk.team/).

  • Participate in 30 Twerk Team Events which include Raids (community or personal). However, at least 5 of these must be Twerk Community Events.

Once you’ve completed all the steps, let TwerkTaco know via Direct Message that you’re ready to submit your application. Remember you must take screenshots of your Tweets and Event/Raid participation to submit. Here’s a little tip - In the Twitch dashboard there's an achievement for the number of raids you've completed, and you can take a screenshot of that achievement progress. It's a road to 100, so get to 25 percent and that counts 25 towards the 30 total events. You will however still require the screenshot proof of the 5 community events.

“And what do I get?” A BUNCH OF COOL STUFF!

  • An exclusive “Partner” Discord Role with pretty gold lettering!

  • A Twerk Team Official Partner Banner

  • A highly coveted spot in the “Partnered” Streamer Gallery on the Twerk Team Website.

  • A SWEET Twerk Team T-shirt guaranteed to arrive before we drop it like it’s hot at TwitchCon 2018!

  • Access to Exclusive Partner Text Channels.

And with our constantly growing and developing community, who knows what else in the future! This is what we’re all about, guys. We’re here to help one another achieve our dreams, and this is just one way to recognise and reward those that are actively demonstrating everything that makes this wonderful community so unique. So let’s all get involved, take up the challenge and spread the love. Small Streamers. Big Hearts!
